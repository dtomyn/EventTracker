Key Elements

  • Heatmap tab in the visualization toolbar (5th button)
  • Year navigation — arrow buttons to page through years
  • GitHub-style grid — 52 week columns × 7 day rows, cyan color scale
  • Tooltip on hover — shows entry count and date
  • Click-to-filter — filtered entry cards appear below the heatmap
  • "Less → More" legend with 5-step cyan gradient
  • Summary stat — total events for the displayed year
